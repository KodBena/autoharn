   declarations too. This is the documented KNOWN OVER-CATCH (bootstrap/templates/led.tmpl's
   own `led obligate` comment): once a principal is obliged, `review_gap` joins on actor
   identity alone, with no time filter — every row that principal ever wrote, before or after
   the obligation existed, counts as debt until individually countersigned. The fixture
   demonstrates this rather than hiding it (also documented in design/

4. GREEN — every one of the six outstanding rows is countersigned by LED_ACTOR=reviewer (a

   plain administrative countersign since they are not themselves mandated-shape discharges.
