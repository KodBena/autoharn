A HAZARD FOUND AND FIXED DURING THIS BUILD, worth banking alongside the refusals above: the
first fixture run's RED-UNAPPLIED-SCHEMA assertion checked for the literal substring "NOT yet
applied" in the script's stdout, but the script's own multi-line `echo` output happened to wrap
that exact phrase across two lines ("is NOT yet" / "   applied on..."), so the live assertion
FAILED even though the underlying behavior was already correct — a fixture bug, not a script
bug, caught by actually running the fixture rather than trusting its logic by inspection (ADR-
0013 Rule 5). Fixed by keeping the phrase "NOT yet applied" together on one `echo` line in
bootstrap/track-experiments.sh; the fixture then passed live on the next run, banked below.
