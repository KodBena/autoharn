## autoharn — Master Synthesis: Sixty Years of Non-Classical Logic, Made Load-Bearing

*A science survey at life-critical seriousness. The unit of analysis is the **obligation**, never the logic. Stakes set required guarantee strength; you assign a formalism because its semantics match a failure mode; everything that gates must be automatable and qualifiable.*

---

## The thesis, restated and judged

**Claim under trial.** The corpus of extended, non-classical, and "merely philosophical" logic developed since roughly 1955 — deontic, epistemic, justification, provability, STIT, defeasible, paraconsistent, substructural, temporal-metric, counterfactual-causal, hyperintensional — can be made *industrially load-bearing* for extreme auditability and deductive maintenance of AI-assisted software at stakes where hundreds of thousands to hundreds of millions of lives are downstream. The enabling change is not a new solver. It is that an LLM now pays the **encoding tax** — the cost of translating a seminar-room logic into a running checker — that historically kept these tools academic. The first act of such a team is `git clone autoharn`.

**Verdict: confirmed, but with a precisely bounded scope and one structural correction.** The thesis is *load-bearing* in the strong sense for a clearly delimited core, *promising* at a frontier, and *honestly ash* in a few named corners — and, critically, the LLM-paid encoding tax is itself the largest new risk, not a free lunch. The synthesis defends three propositions:

1. **Toollessness is not unleverageability.** Of the 27 formal systems surveyed, roughly twenty are runnable *today* on the installed stack (Z3 4.16, clingo 5.8, SWI-Prolog 9.3, OR-Tools CP-SAT, Postgres recursive, OpenJDK 25, JAX/NumPyro) with **zero new dependencies** — the directly-hosted ones (Prolog, ASP, SMT, CP-SAT, probabilistic programming, default/circumscription) plus the ones reached by *standard, lossless encodings* into those hosts (Datalog, modal K–S5 via the standard translation, DEL, STIT choice-cells, AGM revision via `#minimize`, μ-calculus via parity games, FDE via two-bit, HP-causation, bounded MTL/STL via unrolling, GL via well-foundedness). "No dedicated solver" repeatedly resolved to "encode into clingo or Z3," not to a shrug. This is the thesis made concrete.

2. **Different obligations genuinely require different semantics — this is assignment, not a bake-off.** A safety invariant (`INV`) is a `□`/ν-fixpoint over reachable states and wants a model checker or proof assistant; a contrary-to-duty reparation (`DEGRADE`) has *no model-theoretic meaning at all* in any temporal or classical logic and requires dyadic deontic logic; "could have done otherwise" (`ATTR`) is Halpern–Pearl actual causation and nothing else in the taxonomy expresses it; contradiction-tolerance (`CONSIST`) requires the classical base to be *weakened* (paraconsistency), which no amount of SMT muscle supplies. The crown-one-logic instinct fails on contact with the failure-mode catalogue.

3. **The composition — not any single logic — is the novel artifact.** The contribution that makes this a system is a single append-only **claim ledger** with a justification spine, a guarantee-strength meet, and a Gödel–Löb anti-self-certification guardrail, such that the residual risk of a 27-engine composition is one *computed, falsifiable number per claim*, and self-certification is a *structural impossibility* rather than a reviewer's good intention.

**The completeness critic's strongest objection, stated at full force and answered.** The sharpest objection is not "you missed a logic." It is: *every section's kill-condition is some variant of the same failure — a faithful-looking encoding of an unfaithful abstraction, proved green — and the taxonomy contains no obligation naming the adequacy of the specification itself.* The model checker proves the model; the proof assistant proves code-meets-spec; the SMT solver proves whatever it was handed. If an LLM authors both the system-under-analysis's abstraction and the property, the green verdict can be vacuous, and the very review that should catch it (an LLM judging LLM output) *shares the bias* — demonstrated this session when the deflation-detector deflated. A survey that ends 27 times with "the model is on trial" has not discharged its own central risk.

This objection is **correct and is answered structurally, not rhetorically**, in three moves that are themselves the deliverable:

- **The missing meta-obligation is admitted and added.** `SPEC-ADEQ` — the requirement set is itself complete, non-vacuous, and adequate — is a real gap in the original 16-cell taxonomy (the coverage critic is right). autoharn promotes vacuity-detection, spec-mutation, and requirement-completeness to a first-class gate (QUAL-1, QUAL-4, QUAL-5 of the qualification discipline) rather than burying them inside [TRACE](KEY.md#trace) or [INDEP](KEY.md#indep).
- **The abstraction-fidelity gap is a missing *formalism family*, also admitted.** Abstract interpretation (sound over-approximation on real source) and runtime verification (monitor synthesized from spec, replayed against the executable) are the matched answers to "green-on-model ≠ green-on-artifact," and both are absent from the 27. They are added as the conformance gate (QUAL-7). This is the single most important expansion the critic forces.
- **The residual that *cannot* be engineered away is named, not hidden.** Even with perfect encoding qualification, autoharn qualifies an encoding *against a written obligation and an adjudicated fixture corpus*. If the obligation text is wrong or the fixtures are mis-adjudicated, the harness faithfully clears a faithful encoding of the wrong thing. The Löb guardrail forces every justification chain to terminate at a non-LLM source — but a *wrong human axiom still passes*. The honest, non-cheerleading boundary of the thesis is exactly here: **autoharn drives the residual risk down to the quality of human-admitted roots and fixture adjudication, makes that residual a measured quantity (inter-adjudicator agreement), and refuses to ship green when that quantity is below the obligation's required strength** — demoting such obligations to advisory with a logged human gate rather than laundering them. A harness that did otherwise would be the very false-authority failure it exists to prevent.

The thesis survives this objection not by denying it but by converting "the model is on trial" from a confession into a *qualification obligation with mechanical gates* — which is the work of Section 4.

**Prose on the load-bearing assignments.**

*The temporal-safety spine ([INV](KEY.md#inv), [PROG](KEY.md#prog))* is the strongest-covered region and the least controversial. The assignment ladder is by guarantee strength and what is modeled: SAT-BMC is a bounded bug-*finder* (kill it the moment the shallowest real counterexample exceeds feasible depth `k`); TLC/NuSMV are exhaustive over a *finite instance*; mCRL2's μ-calculus reaches infinite-behaviour fixpoints; HOL alone is unbounded over all parameter sizes. The recurring trap across all five is **abstraction fidelity**: a `0..100` integer model of a continuous reservoir says nothing about the float, the sensor noise, or the 700-line C. This is why abstract interpretation and runtime verification are not optional add-ons but the conformance backbone — without them the temporal spine launders green-on-model into green-on-system.

*The norm spine ([TRIG](KEY.md#trig), [DEGRADE](KEY.md#degrade), [AUTH](KEY.md#auth)).* The decisive, non-obvious result is that **contrary-to-duty reparation has no home outside dyadic/CTD deontic logic.** Chisholm's paradox is not a philosopher's curiosity — it is the exact statement that "you ought not X, but if you do X you ought to do Y" is *inconsistent in any logic with monadic obligation*, which is to say a safe-state reaction to an already-violated primary obligation is *undefined* unless you adopt Carmo–Jones actual-vs-ideal-worlds semantics. [DEGRADE](KEY.md#degrade) is the obligation where the "merely philosophical" literature is most directly load-bearing. [AUTH](KEY.md#auth) splits cleanly: permission *closure* is closed-world ASP/circumscription; norm *precedence* is prioritized defaults — both compile to the same `.lp` source and are differentially cross-checked.

*The evidence spine ([PROV](KEY.md#prov), [REVISE](KEY.md#revise), [CONSIST](KEY.md#consist), [CALIB](KEY.md#calib)).* [PROV](KEY.md#prov)'s normal form is Datalog least-fixpoint groundedness — "in the model iff a finite derivation grounds it in primary evidence" — and the superseded-encounter dose-reduction bug dies *before* any numeric check runs, because an ungrounded reading is simply not in the least model. [CONSIST](KEY.md#consist) is where paraconsistency earns its keep: two redundant altimeters disagreeing must yield a *quarantined* `Both` flag routed to degraded mode, never a classically-vacuous clearance for every maneuver. [CALIB](KEY.md#calib) is correctly homed in probabilistic programming with SBC as the mechanical calibration gate — and is the clearest example of the *wrong-bar* failure: a logic invariant relaxed to a probability, or a float bit-pinned, is itself a [CALIB](KEY.md#calib) violation.

*The structural spine ([CLASS](KEY.md#class), [STRUCT](KEY.md#struct), [COHERE](KEY.md#cohere)).* These are the strongest "make-it-unrepresentable" assignments. [CLASS](KEY.md#class) to Description Logic earns its place *only* where `DisjointClasses` + covering + `hasKey` produce a refutation a switch-statement cannot anticipate (multi-axiom subsumption interaction, identity closure); otherwise it is pure encoding tax and the obligation drops to a one-line ASP/SMT MECE check. [STRUCT](KEY.md#struct) to substructural/separation logic and dependent types is the most industrially-landed corner in the entire survey — VeriFast, Iris, and Rust's borrow-checker are the existence proof that this lineage already runs at scale.

*The accountability spine ([ATTR](KEY.md#attr), [COMMIT](KEY.md#commit), [RECORD](KEY.md#record), [INDEP](KEY.md#indep)).* [ATTR](KEY.md#attr) to STIT+Halpern–Pearl is semantically perfect and *input-fragile*: `dstit` requires a modeled choice structure, and if the available alternatives cannot be reconstructed from audit data without authored fiction, the verdict is unfalsifiable theater. [COMMIT](KEY.md#commit) is the **thinnest-covered** obligation — no surveyed system tracks the full commitment lifecycle (created→active→discharged→delegated→cancelled→violated with creditor entitlement) as one object; linear logic nails single-discharge, DEL nails handoff knowledge, directed deontic nails owed-to, but the maintainer composes three by hand, and the matched formalisms (Singh social-commitment protocols, multiparty session types) are absent. [INDEP](KEY.md#indep) is the one obligation discharged *structurally* by a logic that contributes nothing to first-order content: Gödel–Löb provability logic, whose entire payoff is the theorem that self-certification is vacuous.

## The composition: one accountable ledger

The 27 sections answer "which formalism matches this failure mode." Composition answers the question that makes autoharn a *system*: once Z3 has proved a barrier, TLC has exhausted a protocol, clingo has closed a permission set, NumPyro has returned a calibrated tail, and s(CASP) has emitted a deontic justification tree — **what binds these heterogeneous verdicts into one record an auditor can replay, and what stops the binding layer from certifying itself?** Four load-bearing parts, all on the installed Postgres + clingo + Z3 + SWI-Prolog stack.

**1. Substrate — the append-only claim ledger ([RECORD](KEY.md#record) is the floor, not a feature).** Every discharge, from any engine, writes one immutable row: `(obligation_id, kind, artifact_hash, formalism, tool, tool_version, encoding_hash, verdict, certificate, guarantee_strength, recheck_tool, recheck_verdict, witness, decision_clock, authored_at, authored_by)`. Two Postgres invariants make it a ledger, not a log: (a) **append-only with dated sibling-revision** — `REVOKE UPDATE/DELETE` + a `BEFORE` trigger, so a correction inserts a row citing `supersedes` and "what did we believe at decision time" survives by construction ([REVISE](KEY.md#revise)'s append-only and [RECORD](KEY.md#record)'s verbatim demands, discharged mechanically); (b) **criterion-before-result / approval-before-action** — a `CHECK` that the gating row's logical `decision_clock` strictly precedes the action it gates, the mechanical kill for the NYSE clock-skew failure. The logical clock is authoritative because async writes and skew are precisely the attack surface; wall-clock is recorded but never adjudicated on. This is the seam where TLA+'s *ordering-on-the-model* hands off to Postgres's *ordering-on-the-artifact*: the model proves the protocol *can* be ordered, the ledger proves *this run was*.

**2. Spine — justification terms as cross-engine currency.** A bare `pass` is inadmissible. A claim enters only as an Artemov LP justification term `t : C`, checked by a Horn-clause `justifies/2` meta-interpreter in SWI-Prolog over a Postgres EDB of admitted constants. Three operators do the work: **a tool certificate is a leaf, not a root** — Z3's `unsat`, TLC's clean run, clingo's stable model become an *admitted* constant `c:F` only after a diverse oracle re-checks it (cvc5 replays the Z3 proof, µ-toksia re-adjudicates the AF, a second NumPyro seed + SBC re-runs the posterior); this is `!t:(t:F)` made operational. **Application `app(s,t)`** internalizes cross-obligation detachment: when an [INV](KEY.md#inv) barrier proof *uses* a [CALIB](KEY.md#calib) bound as a monitored assumption, the composite witness is `app(s_INV, t_CALIB)` — a first-class, replayable object, not a comment in two disconnected reports. **Sum `s+t`** is the [INDEP](KEY.md#indep) corroboration operator: "two *distinct, separately-checking* justifications exist," required to trace to non-common-cause roots. A forged `app(cite, enc_9999)` whose leaf is not in the store simply fails to re-check — the term cannot lie.

**3. Legibility — the guarantee-strength meet.** [CALIB](KEY.md#calib)'s hardest clause, "confidence that *composes correctly*," is discharged mechanically: a composite claim's effective strength is the **meet (min) over its grounded support graph** — the weakest link governs. On the dam chain `signed_egfr(5) → pore_calibrated(2) → barrier_inv(4) → deploy_permitted → final_clearance`, the verified clingo output is `eff(final_clearance, 2)`: a deploy authorization that *looks* proof-backed inherits the strength of its **calibrated** link, and the ledger gates deploy on `eff ≥ required_strength`. You cannot launder a probabilistic bound into a barrier certificate. **Residual risk is the dual *union*** of each link's modeled-vs-real gap (TLA+ abstraction ∪ SCM faithfulness ∪ prior misspecification) — strength bottlenecks, risk accumulates. **One verified soundness subtlety:** the meet is only sound on the *grounded* subgraph; an injected mutual-admiration cycle returned a spurious `eff=4`. The pipeline order is therefore mandatory and itself a qualification gate: (i) run the Löb guardrail, (ii) delete every `self_certifying` claim, (iii) *then* compute `eff`.

**4. Self-audit — Gödel–Löb as the structural guardrail.** The mission's wound — the deflation-detector that deflated — is defended structurally, not by vigilance. The verified clingo guardrail: `grounded(C) :- external(C). grounded(C) :- support(C,S), grounded(S). self_certifying(C) :- claim(C), not grounded(C).` where `external/1` binds *only* to (a) signed primary inputs and (b) tool certificates that passed a diverse re-check — never a node the producer authored. A laundered `barrier ↔ llm_lemma` cycle flags `self_certifying` for the cycle and everything downstream, while a claim retaining an independent grounded warrant correctly survives. This is Löb operationalized: `□A → A` ("autoharn derived it, therefore it holds") adds nothing; only descent to an unauthored source terminates a chain. Stable-model groundedness *is* the converse-well-founded fixpoint GL's frames require — the guardrail is not an analogy to GL, it is GL's frame condition computed. **The coverage anti-paradox:** autoharn may not prove its own [TRACE](KEY.md#trace) completeness from the ledger's self-report (the `□coverage → coverage` collapse); the mandate enumeration is a *separate signed input* authored by the ratifying authority, and coverage is a *set-difference* (orphan ∪ gap) against a source the prover cannot have written. Gödel's `¬□⊥` is the discipline: exhibit the gap or descend to an external mandate; never self-certify consistency.

**The composition layer is itself on trial** and ships with golden fixtures (pinned clearing/blocking assurance graphs), mutation tests (inject a self-cert cycle → guardrail must flag it and downstream; flip a strength leaf → meet must change; drop a re-check → certificate must become inadmissible), and a differential cross-engine check (same graph in clingo *and* as a Postgres recursive CTE — disagreement disqualifies the encoding, not the obligation). **Architecture kill-condition:** if any laundered cycle escapes the guardrail, OR the meet ever exceeds its weakest grounded link, OR `external/1` is satisfiable by a producer-authored node, the composition is decorative and the ledger is a log with ceremony. Its genuine, non-cheerleading contribution: it makes the residual risk of a 27-engine composition *one computed, falsifiable number per claim*, and makes self-certification a structural impossibility.

## Qualifying the encoding (non-negotiable at these stakes)

The LLM-authored encoding is the largest unmitigated attack surface, and it is dangerous precisely because it *looks like the discharge*. DO-178C/DO-330 supplies the frame: a tool whose output you trust *in lieu of* doing the check yourself must be qualified to a criticality as high as the thing it clears. The qualification question is not "is the system correct?" (that is what the encoding is *for*) but two prior ones — **faithfulness** (does the formal artifact *mean* what the obligation says? a green on a property the real system violates is the lethal case) and **sensitivity** (does it actually *constrain*? a theorem with an unsatisfiable antecedent `Qed`s trivially). Four mechanical techniques attack the faithfulness × sensitivity grid; **the forbidden fifth is LLM-as-judge** — proven this session to ratify the shared blind spot. A model may *propose* fixtures, mutations, or provenance links; the *verdict* on each must be a solver, an execution, a syntactic checker, or a human-admitted axiom.

1. **Differential solving** ([INDEP](KEY.md#indep) backbone): compile the same obligation into two no-common-lineage engines and require identical verdicts — clingo↔Soufflé, Z3↔cvc5, NuSMV↔Spot, CP-SAT↔MiniZinc/Chuffed, clingo-AF↔µ-toksia, HP2SAT↔clingo. Disagreement *disqualifies the encoding*, never "picks the better engine." Necessary, not sufficient — two engines fed the same mistranslation agree on the wrong answer.
2. **Mutation + golden fixtures** (the load-bearing gate): *system-mutation* (seed faults in the system-under-analysis; the encoding must flip green→red on every one) and *encoding-mutation* (delete a clause, weaken a bound, antecedent→False, invariant→True, drop disjointness/fairness/closure; every mutant must break a golden fixture). A surviving encoding-mutant is a dead/vacuous clause — the single most diagnostic gate, because it attacks the vacuity no solver and no second solver can see.
3. **Back-translation**: force a countermodel and require it to be a *recognized hazard instance*; replay a proof/justification and require every leaf to be an admitted axiom or primary datum, re-checked by a *different* engine where possible.
4. **Encoding-as-reading-with-provenance**: every formal clause carries a provenance edge to the obligation-text span it discharges; `:- obligation_clause(C), not covered_by(C,_).` and `:- encoding_clause(E), not justifies(E,_).` must both be UNSAT — coverage is itself a mechanical gate, so a smuggled "comfortable spec" shows up as an unjustified edge.

**The reusable checklist (an encoding passes the *conjunction* before it gates anything):** QUAL-0 toolchain pinned by version+hash (note: `ortools`/`jax` live in a separate venv from system `python3` — pin it). QUAL-1 bidirectional obligation↔clause coverage. QUAL-2 differential agreement ≥2 dissimilar engines. QUAL-3 system-mutation catch-rate (100% on safety-critical seeds). QUAL-4 encoding-mutation sensitivity (every mutant breaks a fixture). QUAL-5 vacuity/non-triviality (antecedent satisfiable, grounding non-empty, bad state reachable). QUAL-6 witness back-translation (countermodel is a real hazard; proof leaves grounded). QUAL-7 conformance to the *artifact* (model ⊑ code via synthesized monitor / abstract interpretation; green-on-model with no refinement is **advisory only**). QUAL-8 guarantee-strength match (logic invariant asserted exactly, not a bounded-`k` BMC sold as `∀`, not a probability; `unknown`/timeout/divergence is **fail-closed**). QUAL-9 independence of the `external` tag (a self-authored "external" node is a Löb violation; ≥2 mis-targeted attempts triggers a mandatory dissimilar-method second channel). QUAL-10 [RECORD](KEY.md#record) (fixtures + thresholds frozen and timestamped *before* the verdict they clear). Passing QUAL-2 while failing QUAL-4 is the classic "two engines agree on a vacuous spec" — which is why the gate is the conjunction.

**The honest residual of the discipline itself:** it qualifies an encoding *against a written obligation and a fixture corpus*; it cannot manufacture a faithful obligation. QUAL-9 forces the chain to a non-LLM root but a wrong *human* axiom still passes. The residual risk is the *adjudication of the fixtures*, and it must be **measured** (inter-adjudicator agreement on the golden set), not assumed. Where that agreement falls below the obligation's required strength, the honest move is to demote the obligation to advisory and route the decision to a human gate with a logged alternative ([ATTR](KEY.md#attr)/dstit) — a harness that reports green regardless of fixture quality is the false-authority failure wearing a uniform.

## Learning path (become broadly expert)

Each rung: the best single resource + the autoharn artifact to build at that rung. Built from the familiar to the deep-philosophical.

**Rung 0 — Deduction as data.** *Abiteboul–Hull–Vianu, Foundations of Databases* (ch. 12–15). Build: a Datalog [PROV](KEY.md#prov)/TRACE check in clingo + Postgres recursive — the superseded-encounter `ungrounded_reduction` relation. You learn least-fixpoint groundedness, the property under everything.

**Rung 1 — Constraint and satisfiability as proof.** *Biere–Heule–van Maaren–Walsh, Handbook of Satisfiability* (2nd ed.) + *Kroening–Strichman, Decision Procedures*. Build: a [CLASS](KEY.md#class) MECE proof in CP-SAT and an [INV](KEY.md#inv) inductive-step in Z3 (the spillway barrier). You learn UNSAT-as-∀-proof and the encoding tax first-hand.

**Rung 2 — Temporal correctness.** *Baier–Katoen, Principles of Model Checking* + *Lamport, Specifying Systems*. Build: the spillway [INV](KEY.md#inv)+[PROG](KEY.md#prog) spec in TLA+/TLC and NuSMV, cross-checked with Spot `ltlcross`. You learn `□`/`◇`, fairness, refinement, and where state explosion bites.

**Rung 3 — The modal substrate.** *Blackburn–de Rijke–Venema, Modal Logic* + *Fitting–Mendelsohn, First-Order Modal Logic*. Build: the standard translation `ST(·,x)` of a `□`-invariant into Z3, with a MetTeL2-generated K-prover as the diverse channel. This is the keystone: every downstream philosophical logic is a modal logic with a special accessibility relation.

**Rung 4 — Calibrated uncertainty.** *Gelman et al., Bayesian Data Analysis* + *Talts–Betancourt, Simulation-Based Calibration*. Build: the NumPyro pore-pressure [CALIB](KEY.md#calib) gate with an SBC kill-condition. You learn why a posterior is the deliverable and how a calibration gate cannot be faked by a passing R̂.

**Rung 5 — Defeasible and non-monotonic reasoning.** *Brewka–Niemelä–Truszczyński, Nonmonotonic Reasoning* (Handbook of KR) + *Gebser et al., Answer Set Solving in Practice*. Build: the [AUTH](KEY.md#auth) closed-world deploy-gate with prioritized-default derogation, plus an AGM piezometer revision in clingo `#minimize`. You learn closure, exceptions, and retraction propagation.

**Rung 6 — Norms as first-class objects.** *Gabbay et al., Handbook of Deontic Logic, Vols. 1–2* + *Carmo–Jones, "Deontic Logic and Contrary-to-Duties."* Build: the s(CASP) contrary-to-duty revert obligation with a justification tree. You learn why Chisholm's paradox is an engineering failure mode and how [DEGRADE](KEY.md#degrade) gets a defined semantics.

**Rung 7 — Evidence, agency, provenance.** *Artemov–Fitting, Justification Logic* + *Halpern, Actual Causality* + *Belnap–Perloff–Xu, Facing the Future*. Build: the `justifies/2` spine, an HP2SAT/Z3 Fed-limit attribution with a witness certificate, and a STIT `dstit` choice-cell gate. You learn proof-carrying belief, "could have done otherwise," and the difference between authorship and coincidence.

**Rung 8 — Conflict, resource, and the self-referential guardrail.** *Priest, Introduction to Non-Classical Logic* (FDE/LP) + *Reynolds, "Separation Logic"* + *Boolos, The Logic of Provability*. Build: the FDE two-bit sensor-conflict containment in Z3, a VeriFast linear settlement-leg, and the clingo Löb guardrail. You learn paraconsistent quarantine, resource discipline, and why no producer may certify itself.

**Rung 9 — The qualification discipline and the frontier.** *RTCA DO-178C / DO-330* + *Ammann–Offutt, Introduction to Software Testing* (mutation) + *Cousot, "Abstract Interpretation"* + *Fine, "Truthmaker Semantics."* Build: the full QUAL-0..10 harness over a corpus of seeded-pathology encodings, then a speculative truthmaker-relevance [PROV](KEY.md#prov) experiment. You learn to qualify the qualifier and to recognize honest ash by genuine search.

## Honest verdicts & the first experiment

Per-formalism, each with a kill-condition. **Load-bearing** = semantics match, tool runs, leverage is structural and falsifiable. **Promising** = real match, one fragility (usually input-authoring or scale). **Ash by argument** = does not earn its keep beyond a cheaper substitute for *this* purpose.

**Load-bearing.** *Datalog/Deductive DB* ([PROV](KEY.md#prov)/TRACE — their normal form). *Prolog/CLP* ([PROV](KEY.md#prov), AUTH-closure, and the universal encoding host). *ASP/clingo* ([AUTH](KEY.md#auth), [CLASS](KEY.md#class) — closed-world closure and MECE are what stable models compute; also the load-bearing encoding host for 11+ toolless logics). *SMT/Z3+cvc5* ([INV](KEY.md#inv), [STRUCT](KEY.md#struct), [COHERE](KEY.md#cohere), [CLASS](KEY.md#class), and the bias-independent oracle for [CALIB](KEY.md#calib)/INDEP). *SAT/CP-SAT* ([CLASS](KEY.md#class), CONSIST-with-MUS, [STRUCT](KEY.md#struct) range-safety — UNSAT is a genuine ∀-proof). *LTL/CTL model checking* and *TLA+/TLC* ([INV](KEY.md#inv)/PROG over finite-control protocols). *μ-calculus/mCRL2* ([INV](KEY.md#inv)/PROG over infinite behaviour). *MTL/STL* ([PROG](KEY.md#prog), [TRIG](KEY.md#trig), FTTI — robustness gives a signed margin). *HOL/proof assistants* ([INV](KEY.md#inv)/STRUCT/CALIB/INDEP on the small catastrophic core; the kernel is the canonical independent adjudicator). *Probabilistic programming* ([CALIB](KEY.md#calib) — posterior + SBC). *Paraconsistent FDE/LP* ([CONSIST](KEY.md#consist) — non-triviality is a theorem). *Substructural/separation* ([STRUCT](KEY.md#struct)/COMMIT/COHERE — the most industrially-landed lineage). *Deontic/CTD* ([TRIG](KEY.md#trig)/DEGRADE/AUTH/CONSIST — the only home for contrary-to-duty). *Provability Logic/Löb* ([INDEP](KEY.md#indep) — the structural anti-self-certification guardrail, cheap and runnable). Kill-conditions per section above; the shared one is the conformance kill (green-on-model not refining the artifact).

**Promising (real, one fragility).** *Description Logic* — load-bearing only where `DisjointClasses`+covering+`hasKey` produce a refutation a switch-statement cannot; **kill** if every catch reduces to a one-line enum check. *STIT/Halpern–Pearl [ATTR](KEY.md#attr)* — semantically perfect, **kill** if choice cells / structural equations cannot be recovered from audit data without authored fiction (input-fragile). *Belief Revision/AGM* — operator is sound, **kill** if entrenchment orderings cannot be elicited stably (different qualified maintainers flip ≥X% of safety verdicts), in which case a coarser append-only log serves [REVISE](KEY.md#revise) better. *Defeasible/argumentation* — **kill** if seeded *encoding* mutations survive dual-solver agreement (solver agreement alone never clears the bar; only mutation-detection on the encoding does). *DEL/epistemic* — **kill** if every "handoff" reduces to a single-writer fact already caught by [COHERE](KEY.md#cohere)/RECORD; survives iff genuine lost-acknowledgment / mutual-knowledge structure appears. *ILP* — generator-of-candidates only; **kill** if recovery explodes at realistic arity or fails to beat a frequency baseline, and *common-cause kill* if examples come from the artifact under test. *Counterfactual/causal* — **kill** if LLM-authored SCMs score <90% on adjudicated actual-cause benchmarks. *Hyperintensional frontier* — free logic ([STRUCT](KEY.md#struct)) and dependence logic ([COHERE](KEY.md#cohere)) already run; truthmaker/grounding are genuinely speculative.

**Ash by argument (honest, by genuine search).** *Justification Logic as a logic* — Datalog why-provenance delivers checkable, forgery-resistant, retraction-respecting chains with equal strength; **survives only** if a production case needs object-level reasoning *about* justification terms (`s+t` cross-channel corroboration as a first-class assertion) — which the composition spine *does* exhibit, narrowly rescuing it there. *Provability Logic's modal axiomatics* — the *principle* (no self-certification) is load-bearing; GL's axioms reduce to an acyclicity check on the assurance graph — ash as tooling, gold as principle. *Truthmaker semantics & grounding* — high ash risk; **kill** if exact-relevance does not separate genuine warrants from idle-conjunct confabulations at ≥0.9 precision/recall over classical-entailment+citation-check. *Fuzzy/t-norm for [CALIB](KEY.md#calib)* — truth-functional, not probabilistic; ash for confidence propagation, legitimate only for vagueness. *Dialetheism qua metaphysics* — FDE's four-valued bookkeeping is the engineering leverage; "some contradictions are true" buys nothing operationally. *MLN/PSL dedicated tooling* — pracmln/Tuffy unmaintained; semantics re-homed into clingo weak constraints. *ILP as a guarantee* — ash anywhere a guarantee is owed.

**THE single highest-leverage first experiment.** *The meta-qualification corpus.* Assemble **N ≥ 50 LLM-authored encodings across ≥5 formalisms** (an [INV](KEY.md#inv) in Z3+TLC, an [AUTH](KEY.md#auth) in clingo, a [CALIB](KEY.md#calib) in NumPyro, a [COMMIT](KEY.md#commit) in VeriFast, a [CONSIST](KEY.md#consist) in FDE/Z3), each in two variants: faithful, and one with a *single seeded pathology* drawn from a pre-registered set — vacuous antecedent, weakened bound, dropped disjointness/fairness/closure, antecedent→False, invariant→True, a self-authored "external" tag, a retrofitted criterion. Run the full QUAL-0..10 conjunction with all verdicts rendered by solver/execution/syntactic-checker/admitted-axiom (no LLM in any verdict path). **Success:** every seeded pathology is caught by ≥1 mechanical gate, and every faithful encoding clears all ten, with the catch attributable to a *named, non-LLM* gate. **Kill (the discipline is decorative):** any seeded pathology clears all ten green, OR any catch secretly required a model's judgment, OR — the deeper kill — fixture adjudication on realistic obligations cannot reach inter-adjudicator agreement above the residual-risk budget, in which case those obligations must ship advisory, not green. This experiment is highest-leverage because it puts the *thesis's own central risk* (the LLM-paid encoding tax) on trial first; every other formalism's value is conditional on this passing.

## Open questions (genuine forks for the maintainer)

1. **The fixture-adjudication floor.** Every gate terminates at human-admitted roots and an adjudicated corpus. What is the *measured* inter-adjudicator agreement on safety-critical fixtures, and is there any obligation class where it is structurally unreachable (novel oncology presentations, genuinely contested norms)? If so, autoharn's honest output there is "advisory + logged human gate," not green — but *which* obligations fall below the floor is an empirical question only the maintainer's domain can answer.

2. **The three structural gaps the mission's own stakes make non-negotiable.** *Information-flow confidentiality* (a 2-safety hyperproperty — PHI/MNPI/position data not flowing to lower clearance — that no single-trace checker in the roster expresses; needs security type systems / HyperLTL/MCHyper, all absent locally). *The artifact-fidelity bridge* (abstract interpretation + runtime verification — the only sound answers to "green-on-model ≠ green-on-system," which every temporal section currently only confesses). *The cryptographic half of [RECORD](KEY.md#record)* (tamper-evident, provable happens-before across async writes — a distributed-systems + Merkle/hash-chain problem no logic family owns). Does autoharn ship without these, flagged, or wait?

3. **Hyperproperties as the deeper missing layer.** [INDEP](KEY.md#indep) *gestures* at channel diversity, but "redundant channels given identical inputs produce identical safety outputs" is a relation over *sets* of traces (Clarkson–Schneider 2-safety), inexpressible in any single-trace formalism here. Is real verifier independence itself a hyperproperty autoharn must check with HyperLTL — and if the redundant channels share an LLM-authored spec defect, has the entire qualification edifice a common cause it cannot see from inside?

4. **[COMMIT](KEY.md#commit)'s untooled composition.** The thinnest obligation requires hand-composing linear logic + DEL + directed deontic, none qualified together. Do session types / Singh social-commitment protocols (absent from the roster) deserve a 28th section, or is the linear-resource VeriFast encoding plus a commitment state-machine sufficient at stakes?

5. **The DO-178C tool-qualification status of autoharn itself.** The composition layer, the spine, the meet, and the guardrail are all LLM-authorable code. They ship with golden fixtures and mutation tests — but is that *enough* qualification evidence for a harness gating dam spillway or NASA flight software, or does autoharn's own kernel need an independent, human-authored re-implementation as the diverse channel (the seL4/CompCert standard) before it may gate anything lethal?

6. **Where does the meet's monotonicity break under real composition?** The strength-meet is verified sound only on the grounded subgraph after cycle deletion. Are there legitimate *non-monotonic* compositions (a defeasible argument strengthening on new evidence) where weakest-link is the wrong aggregator, and does the residual-risk *union* ever undercount a correlated failure across two "independent" links that share a hidden LLM-authored abstraction?
